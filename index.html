Why Fitness 19?

Fitness 19 was founded by fitness industry veterans who believed that the traditional health club model wasn’t meeting the needs of most people. The result is a simple fitness club concept – an extremely affordable, family-friendly facility that offers state-of-the-art cardio, strength, and free weight equipment.

Classes

Many locations offer your favorite group classes to help you reach your personal fitness goals, including Zumba, Boot Camp, Yoga, Pilates, Spinn & Insanity. Please check with your local club for details and class schedules.

Month to Month Memberships

Our month-to-month memberships offer you the convenience and flexibility of no long-term contracts. Pre-paid memberships are also available in a variety of terms from 30 days to 1 year.

Personal Training

Whatever your personal fitness goals, our trainers can show you the most efficient path to reaching them. Benefit from the expertise of professionals who have the training and experience to guide you toward success.
